Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This site sells ebooks directly to readers. This policy explains what information is collected during that process and how it's used.

What we collect

When you check out, we collect the email address you provide and the items in your order. Payment card details are entered directly into Paystack's secure payment popup and are never seen or stored by this site.

How it's used

Your email is used to send your order confirmation and download links, and to reach you if there's a problem with your order. It is not sold or shared with third parties for marketing.

What we store

Order records (email, items purchased, amount, payment status) are kept so download links can be verified and reissued if needed. Download links are unique, time-limited, and tied to a specific paid order.

Third parties

Payments are processed by Paystack. Ebook files are hosted on Cloudinary. Emails are sent via Resend. Each provider has its own privacy policy governing data that passes through their systems.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data can be sent to the contact address listed on the About page.
